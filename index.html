About Me

Greetings! I'm a dedicated web developer with a passion for crafting seamless experiences in both front-end and back-end technologies.

Originally hailing from Ukraine, I now proudly call the vibrant city of Berlin my home. Embarking on my programming journey, I'm thrilled to channel my creativity and problem-solving skills into the ever-evolving tech industry. In my earlier chapters, I wore multiple hats, serving as a mathematics teacher and the proud owner of an educational company. Our mission was clear - providing comprehensive educational courses to guide students on their journey to university admissions. Leading a team of up to 10 individuals, we fostered an environment of learning and growth.

My leadership journey took a transformative turn when I decided to create a unique platform for students. From collaborating with talented programmers and designers to orchestrating processes, web optimization, and strategic advertisement, every step was a testament to innovation. Explore our platform here.

Before this chapter, I dedicated several enriching years to traversing the globe. My adventures took me to over 60 countries, and during this time, I found myself aboard a cruise ship. Imagine a world cruise, where my role at the front desk as a receptionist allowed me to contribute to the enchanting guest experience.
